# Go-to-Market Strategy
## Public AI Switzerland

- Build awareness with a clear public narrative.
- Convert users to members through immediate utility.
- Expand institutional adoption where Swiss hosting is decisive.

---

## GTM pillar 1
## Narrative and legitimacy

- Physical zine for launch storytelling.
- Weekly blog and media cadence.
- Presence in trusted community channels.

---

## GTM pillar 2
## Developer relations

- Strong docs and implementation guides.
- Demos, workshops, and hackathons.
- Partner integrations to seed downstream usage.

---

## GTM pillar 3
## Institutional sales

- Target sectors with compliance and residency requirements.
- Prioritize pilots that prove reliability and governance fit.
- Convert early pilots into long-term contracts.

---

## Success signals

- Member conversion from active users.
- Growth in API usage among SMEs and startups.
- New institutional pilots and renewals.
