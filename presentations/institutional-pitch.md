# Institutional Pitch
## Swiss-Governed AI Infrastructure

- Built for public and regulated organizations.
- Swiss-hosted deployment with local governance.
- Transparent operating model for long-term trust.

---

## Core value proposition

- Data residency in Switzerland.
- Open standards and audit-friendly architecture.
- Predictable pricing and stable access pathways.

---

## Why now

- AI is becoming core infrastructure.
- Switching costs rise as platforms embed into workflows.
- Early adoption secures long-term sovereignty and flexibility.

---

## Delivery model

- API access for technical teams.
- Chat interface for non-technical staff.
- Cooperative governance with clear operational accountability.

---

## Pilot structure

- 8 to 12 week implementation window.
- Joint definition of use cases and success metrics.
- Path to production based on reliability and compliance fit.
