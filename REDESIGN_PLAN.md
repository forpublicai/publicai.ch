## 2. INTRODUCTION / PROBLEM-SOLUTION NARRATIVE
**Current**: Long paragraphs about Apertus and Public AI concept
**New**: Concise problem-solution story following Subvert's pattern

**Structure:**
- **Problem Statement**: 
  - Switzerland relies on foreign AI platforms (Silicon Valley/Shenzhen)
  - Global platforms optimized for scale, not local norms/industries/communities
  - Apertus exists but needs reliable hosting, tooling, and distribution
  
- **Solution Statement**:
  - Public AI Switzerland aggregates demand through a nation-wide cooperative
  - Instead of CHF 240/year for ChatGPT, members pay CHF 100 to own their AI
  - Transparent overages, surplus reinvested in capacity/prices/community
  - Distribution network extends beyond inference for other Swiss-made tech

- **Why Now Section**:
  - Swiss cooperative tradition (Migros, Coop, Raiffeisen, Mobility)
  - AI is now critical infrastructure, but control consolidating around foreign platforms
  - Switzerland has ingredients: sovereign model (Apertus), data protection law, trust in co-ops
  - Bottleneck is distribution/governance/trust—gap is narrow enough to close now
